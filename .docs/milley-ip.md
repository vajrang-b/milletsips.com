# Milley IP (The Millet Stalk Character)

## Character Definition
Milley is the official brand design character for **Millet Sip**. Milley is designed specifically to resemble the wobbly, hand-drawn version of a millet stalk (panicle), directly inspired by the natural plant anatomy (an elongated cluster of seeds with curved leaves on a thin stem), representing authentic organic heritage.

## Appearance
- **Shape:** A vertical slender stem/stalk with two prominent long, curved leaves arching outwards from it.
- **Top Panicle:** At the top of the stem, there is an elongated, vertical cylindrical cluster (ear) densely packed with tiny wobbly circles/grains, complete with stippled textures.
- **Face:** Two simple black dot eyes placed in the upper half of the elongated seed cluster.
- **Limbs:** Thin, wobbly stick arms and legs extending outwards from the vertical stem beneath the leaves.
- **Body Color:** Clean white/transparent body with wobbly black ink lines.
- **Expression:** Deadpan, calm, and focused.

## Personality
- Earnest, natural, grounded, and hardworking.
- Passionate about health, heritage grains, and sustainable lifestyles.

## Visual Prompts Guide (For Qai-Style Generation)
When prompting the illustration model for Milley, include the exact plant structural references:
- **Prompt description:** `"hand-drawn illustration, solo, single character only, a wobbly vertical millet plant stalk with two curved leaves extending from the stem, a long textured cylinder of clustered seeds on top of the stalk, wobbly thin black outline, two small black dot eyes on the top seed cluster, thin stick limbs"`
- **Prohibited:** No simple teardrops, no lemons, no human faces.
