The Healthy Third Space

Reclaiming ancient nutrition for the modern day hustle.

At Millet Sips, we position ourselves at the intersection of traditional home-cooked heritage and fast-paced urban convenience. We believe that office energy shouldn't depend on sugary chemical shots or artificial syrups.

We harvest premium local millets (Ragi, Foxtail, Pearl, and Kodo), wash them with pure mountain water, and slow-brew them with cardamom, mint, and raw honeys to deliver a clean, sustained energy release.

Milley filters out the artificial sugars and heavy caffeines, swapping them for clean, organic energy.
