Standard Franchise Operational Procedure

Our step-by-step roadmap to launch your franchise partner journey.

1
Registration Application: Complete the registration form online or offline with a processing fee of Rs. 499.
2
Review & Call Back: Within 24 hours, our operational team reviews your application and schedules a one-to-one meeting within 3 working days.
3
Legal Agreement: Sign the legal franchise agreement on the meeting day or within 3 working days, with a non-refundable deposit of Rs. 50,000.
4
Site Selection: Within 48 hours of signing the MoU, site selection begins along with franchise location layout suggestions.
5
First Establishment Payment: Deposit 80% of the franchise establishment amount to start construction and hardware setup.
6
Technical Training: Within 3 working days of payment, technical training begins (2 days at company kitchen or training center).
7
Franchise Unit Setup: Complete hardware delivery and kiosk installation within 5 to 15 working days.
8
Balance Payment: Deposit the remaining 20% of the franchise establishment amount.
9
Onsite Handholding: 3 days of intensive onsite training at your new franchise establishment location or kiosk.
10
Inauguration Ceremony: Launch event, opening day ceremony, along with local promotional support and PR marketing.
11
Active handholding: Up to 3 months of company-supported branding, marketing, and operational help.
12
Growth Partnership: Continuous menu innovation, feedback loops, and expansion partnership.
